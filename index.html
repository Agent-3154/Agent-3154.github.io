Botian XU (许博添)

Hi, there. I am currently a PhD student at CUHK MAE. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from SUSTech in 2022. After that, I spent 3 gap years at Tsinghua University, with Prof. Yi Wu and Prof. Huazhe Xu, working on reinforcement learning and robotics.

Interests:

  • Reinforcement Learning for Legged Robots
  • 3D Modeling and Perception for Robot Agents

I am lucky to work closely with Liu Cao, Qingzhou Lu, and Haoyang Weng.
